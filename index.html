Samson Phan

Samson Hao Phan (born April 13, 2004) is a college student currently attending Penn State Berks. He is majoring in IST. Samson is currently serving as the SGA officer for the Berks Chess Club.


Early Life

Samson was born in Boston, Massachusetts. His parents were both born in Vietnam. Not too long after being born, his family would move down to Reading, Pennsylvania. He grew up with his older brother, Steven.

Later, Samson's family would move closer to the Leesport, where he attended Schuylkill Valley High School. He graduated high school in 2022.

Personal Life

In elementary school, he learned how to play the viola. However, after moving and attending Schuylkill Valley, he would change to playing the french horn. He joined a D&D club at his high school and a chess club in college.
